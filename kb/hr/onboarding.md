# Employee Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the team! This guide will help you navigate your first days and weeks at the company.

---

## Before Your First Day

### What We're Preparing for You

- Laptop and equipment (shipped to your address or available at office)
- Email and system accounts
- Slack workspace access
- Calendar invitations for onboarding sessions
- Benefits enrollment information

### What You Should Prepare

- **Government ID** for I-9 verification (passport OR driver's license + Social Security card)
- **Voided check or bank details** for direct deposit setup
- **Emergency contact information**
- Questions you'd like to ask!

## First 30 Days

### Goals

By the end of your first 30 days, you should:

- Understand your role and key responsibilities
- Know your team members and stakeholders
- Complete all required training
- Have access to all necessary systems
- Understand current projects and priorities
- Complete your first meaningful contribution
- Have regular 1:1s scheduled with your manager

### 30-Day Milestones

| Week | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| Week 1 | Complete onboarding, meet team, set up systems |
| Week 2 | Shadow team members, start small tasks |
| Week 3 | Take ownership of defined tasks, attend client meetings |
| Week 4 | Complete first deliverable, 30-day check-in with manager |

### Meetings to Schedule

- **Manager 1:1:** Weekly (your manager will set up)
- **Team meetings:** Join standing meetings
- **Skip-level:** Meet your manager's manager within 30 days
- **Cross-functional:** Meet key partners from other teams
- **Mentor/Buddy:** Regular check-ins if assigned

---

## First 90 Days

### 60-Day Checkpoint

By day 60, you should:
- Be contributing independently to projects
- Understand team processes and workflows
- Have built relationships across the team
- Identified areas for growth and development
- Received initial feedback from manager

### 90-Day Review

At 90 days, you'll have a formal check-in covering:
- Performance against initial expectations
- Feedback from manager and peers
- Goal setting for the next quarter
- Professional development planning
- Any concerns or support needed

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: When do my benefits start?**
A: Benefits coverage begins the first of the month following your start date. You have 30 days to enroll.

**Q: How do I request time off?**
A: Submit requests through the HRIS (Workday). See the [time off policy](time_off.md) for details.

**Q: What if I have IT issues?**
A: Post in #it-help on Slack or email it@company.com. For urgent issues, call the IT hotline.

**Q: How do I submit expenses?**
A: Use Expensify. See the [expense policy](expense_reimbursement.md) for guidelines.

**Q: Who do I contact for HR questions?**
A: Email hr@company.com or post in #hr-help on Slack.

**Q: When is my 90-day review?**
A: Your manager will schedule this. It typically occurs during week 12-13.

**Q: How do I access the office after hours?**
A: Your badge provides 24/7 access. Contact Facilities if you have issues.
