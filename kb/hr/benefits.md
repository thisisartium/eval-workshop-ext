# Employee Benefits

This document outlines the company's benefits programs available to eligible employees.

---

## Eligibility

| Employment Type | Benefits Eligibility |
|-----------------|---------------------|
| Full-time (30+ hours/week) | All benefits |
| Part-time (20-29 hours/week) | Limited benefits (noted below) |
| Contractors/Temps | Not eligible |

Benefits coverage begins on the **first of the month** following your start date.

## Health Insurance

### Medical Plans

The company offers three medical plan options through Anthem Blue Cross:

| Plan | Monthly Premium (Employee) | Monthly Premium (Family) | Deductible | Out-of-Pocket Max |
|------|---------------------------|-------------------------|------------|-------------------|
| PPO Premium | $150 | $450 | $500 | $3,000 |
| PPO Standard | $75 | $250 | $1,500 | $5,000 |
| HDHP + HSA | $25 | $100 | $3,000 | $6,500 |

**Company Contribution:** The company pays approximately 80% of premium costs. Amounts shown are employee contributions.

### Plan Features

**PPO Plans:**
- No referrals required for specialists
- Nationwide provider network
- Prescription drug coverage included
- Preventive care covered at 100%

**HDHP + HSA:**
- Lower premiums, higher deductible
- Company contributes **$750/year** (individual) or **$1,500/year** (family) to your HSA
- HSA funds roll over year to year
- Triple tax advantage: pre-tax contributions, tax-free growth, tax-free qualified withdrawals

### Dependent Coverage

You may enroll the following dependents:
- Legal spouse or domestic partner
- Children under age 26 (biological, adopted, step, or foster)
- Disabled dependents (any age, with documentation)

## 401(k) Retirement Plan

### Plan Highlights

- **Eligibility:** Immediately upon hire
- **Provider:** Fidelity Investments
- **Contribution limit:** IRS maximum ($23,000 in 2024; $30,500 if age 50+)

### Company Match

The company matches **100% of the first 4%** of salary you contribute.

| Your Contribution | Company Match | Total |
|-------------------|---------------|-------|
| 0% | 0% | 0% |
| 2% | 2% | 4% |
| 4% | 4% | 8% |
| 6% | 4% | 10% |

### Vesting Schedule

- **Employee contributions:** Always 100% vested
- **Company match:** 3-year graded vesting
  - Year 1: 33% vested
  - Year 2: 66% vested
  - Year 3: 100% vested

### Investment Options

- Target-date funds (default based on expected retirement year)
- Index funds (S&P 500, Total Market, International, Bond)
- Actively managed funds
- Self-directed brokerage option

### Roth 401(k)

A Roth 401(k) option is available for after-tax contributions with tax-free qualified withdrawals in retirement.

## Life and Disability Insurance

### Basic Life Insurance

- **Coverage:** 1x annual salary (up to $500,000)
- **Cost:** 100% company-paid
- **Beneficiary:** Designate via benefits portal

### Supplemental Life Insurance

- **Coverage:** Up to 5x annual salary (max $1,000,000)
- **Cost:** Employee-paid, competitive group rates
- **Guarantee issue:** Up to 3x salary without medical underwriting (if elected within 30 days of hire)

### Accidental Death & Dismemberment (AD&D)

- **Coverage:** Equal to life insurance amount
- **Cost:** 100% company-paid for basic; optional supplemental available

### Short-Term Disability (STD)

- **Benefit:** 60% of weekly salary
- **Duration:** Up to 12 weeks
- **Waiting period:** 7 days
- **Cost:** 100% company-paid

### Long-Term Disability (LTD)

- **Benefit:** 60% of monthly salary (max $10,000/month)
- **Duration:** Until age 65 or recovery
- **Waiting period:** 90 days
- **Cost:** 100% company-paid

## Enrollment and Changes

### Open Enrollment

- **When:** November 1-15 each year
- **Effective:** January 1 of the following year
- Review and update your elections annually

### Qualifying Life Events

You may change benefits outside open enrollment within **30 days** of:

- Marriage or divorce
- Birth or adoption of a child
- Loss of other coverage
- Change in employment status
- Moving to a new coverage area

### How to Enroll

1. Log into the benefits portal at [benefits.company.com]
2. Review plan options and costs
3. Add/verify dependents (documentation may be required)
4. Confirm elections and beneficiaries
5. Save confirmation for your records
