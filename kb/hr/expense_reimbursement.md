# Expense Reimbursement Policy

This policy outlines guidelines for business-related expenses and the reimbursement process.

---

## General Principles

- Expenses must be **reasonable, necessary, and business-related**
- Obtain **pre-approval** for expenses over $500
- Submit expenses within **30 days** of incurring them
- Provide **itemized receipts** for all expenses over $25
- The company reserves the right to deny reimbursement for expenses that violate this policy

---

## Travel Expenses

### Air Travel

| Class | Policy |
|-------|--------|
| Domestic flights under 4 hours | Economy class |
| Domestic flights 4+ hours | Economy Plus/Comfort+ allowed |
| International flights under 6 hours | Economy Plus/Comfort+ allowed |
| International flights 6+ hours | Business class with VP approval |

**Booking Guidelines:**
- Book at least 14 days in advance when possible
- Use the company's preferred travel booking tool (Navan/TripActions)
- Choose refundable fares only when schedule is uncertain
- Loyalty program credits accrue to the employee

**Baggage:**
- One checked bag reimbursed for trips 3+ days
- Additional bags require justification (equipment, materials)

### Ground Transportation

| Type | Policy |
|------|--------|
| Rental cars | Mid-size or smaller; full-size for 3+ travelers |
| Rideshare (Uber/Lyft) | Allowed; use shared rides when practical |
| Personal vehicle | $0.67/mile (2024 IRS rate) |
| Parking | Actual cost with receipt |
| Tolls | Actual cost with receipt |

**Rental Car Guidelines:**
- Decline supplemental insurance (covered by corporate policy)
- Refuel before returning to avoid fuel surcharges
- Economy/compact preferred for solo travel

### Lodging

| Location | Nightly Limit |
|----------|---------------|
| Tier 1 cities (NYC, SF, LA, Boston, DC) | $300 |
| Tier 2 cities (Chicago, Seattle, Denver, Austin) | $225 |
| All other locations | $175 |

**Guidelines:**
- Book standard rooms; suites require VP approval
- Use corporate rates at preferred hotels when available
- Stay at client-recommended hotels when requested
- Incidentals (minibar, movies, room service) are not reimbursable unless meals

**Extended Stay (5+ nights):**
- Consider Airbnb or extended stay hotels
- Weekly rates often provide savings
- Requires manager approval

## Meals and Entertainment

### Per Diem Option

Employees may elect per diem instead of itemized meal reimbursement:

| Meal | Per Diem Amount |
|------|-----------------|
| Breakfast | $20 |
| Lunch | $25 |
| Dinner | $40 |
| **Daily Total** | **$85** |

- Per diem does not require receipts
- Cannot combine per diem and itemized meals for same trip
- Per diem reduced by 50% if meals are provided (conference, client)

### Itemized Meals

If submitting actual receipts instead of per diem:

| Meal Type | Limit (per person) |
|-----------|--------------------|
| Breakfast | $25 |
| Lunch | $30 |
| Dinner | $60 |
| Coffee/snacks | $15/day |

**Guidelines:**
- Alcohol limited to 2 drinks with dinner; not reimbursable without a client present
- Tips should not exceed 20%
- Room service follows meal limits above

### Client Entertainment

| Activity | Limit | Approval Required |
|----------|-------|-------------------|
| Client meals | $150/person | No |
| Client meals | $150-300/person | Manager |
| Client meals | $300+/person | VP |
| Team dinners | $100/person | Manager |
| Sporting events/shows | $250/person | VP |

**Documentation Required:**
- Itemized receipt
- Names and titles of attendees
- Business purpose
- Client company name

- Conference registration fees
- Course and certification fees
- Professional books and materials

- Workshop and seminar fees

**Process:**
1. Get manager approval before registering

**Travel for Conferences:**

- Counts against travel budget, not PD budget

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are **not eligible** for reimbursement:

- Personal travel or entertainment
- Traffic violations and parking tickets
- Airline club memberships (unless pre-approved for heavy travelers)
- Travel insurance (covered by corporate policy)
- Childcare or pet care during travel
- Clothing or personal items
- Personal phone calls (beyond reasonable)
- Upgrades without documented justification
- Alcohol without client present
- Expenses for non-employees (except approved client entertainment)
- Late fees or interest charges
- Expenses older than 60 days

## Submission Process

### How to Submit Expenses

1. **Log into** the expense system (Expensify/Concur) within 30 days
2. **Upload receipts** - photo or PDF of itemized receipt
3. **Categorize** the expense correctly
4. **Add details:**
   - Business purpose
   - Project/client code (if applicable)
   - Attendees (for meals/entertainment)
5. **Submit** for approval

### Receipt Requirements

| Expense Amount | Documentation Required |
|----------------|----------------------|
| Under $25 | No receipt required (meals, transit) |
| $25 - $75 | Receipt or credit card statement |
| Over $75 | Itemized receipt required |
| All lodging | Itemized hotel folio |
| All airfare | Booking confirmation + receipt |

### Approval Workflow

| Expense Total | Approver |
|---------------|----------|
| Under $500 | Manager |
| $500 - $2,500 | Director |
| $2,500 - $10,000 | VP |
| Over $10,000 | CFO |

### Reimbursement Timeline

- **Submission deadline:** Within 30 days of expense
- **Approval SLA:** Within 5 business days
- **Payment:** Next payroll cycle after approval (direct deposit)

## Corporate Card

### Eligibility

Corporate cards are issued to employees who:
- Travel frequently (4+ trips per year)
- Regularly incur business expenses
- Have manager recommendation

### Usage Guidelines

- Use **only** for business expenses
- Personal charges are **strictly prohibited**
- Lost/stolen cards: Report immediately to Finance
- Reconcile expenses within 14 days of statement

### Card Benefits

- No personal liability for business expenses
- Travel insurance and purchase protection
- Rewards accrue to company account

## International Travel

### Pre-Trip Requirements

- Register trip in the travel security portal
- Verify passport validity (6+ months)
- Check visa requirements
- Review country-specific per diem rates (GSA rates)

### Currency and Exchange

- Use corporate card for best exchange rates
- ATM withdrawals for cash (reimbursable fees)
- Document exchange rate used if paying cash
- Convert to USD using date-of-expense rate

### International Per Diem

International per diem follows U.S. Department of State rates, which vary by city. Check the [GSA per diem lookup](https://www.gsa.gov/travel/plan-book/per-diem-rates) before travel.
